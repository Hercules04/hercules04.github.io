My Transition Year ExperienceBy Adam Potterton

Gaisce

Since the start of the year I have been completing the bronze award for An Gaisce. The award requires you to do a personal skill activity for thirteen weeks, a physical recreation activity for thirteen weeks and a community involvement activity for thirteen weeks. You must do each activity for at least one hour each week. Then you must choose one activity to do for a further thirteen weeks. There is also an adventure journey to be completed, which is a two day one night journey where you walk between 25-30 kilometres or cycle between 100-130 kilometres.

For my personal skill, I started learning the guitar. The guitar is an instrument I had always wanted to play and this seemed like the perfect opportunity to learn. I started taking lessons on Wednesday evenings which I am still attending after the required thirteen weeks.

For community involvement I was a junior churchwarden at my local church.
