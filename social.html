Social Volunteering

Run in the Dark

On 16th November, 2016, I took part in the Run in the Dark. This is a nighttime run around Dublin. The event is a fundraising activity to find a cure for spinal cord injury. The charity behind the event is the Mark Pollock Trust. This trust was set up by a man called Mark Pollock who was the first blind man to venture to the South Pole. Mark had come in to the school to speak to us earlier in the year.
